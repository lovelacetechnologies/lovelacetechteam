Tutorials

Like some those who have stumbled upon my corner of the internet, I am terminally online and addicted to this technology. I have a tutorial series I am working on that addresses this addiction and the ways I have managed to solve this. Some places online really suck you into a vortex of pain and sadness. The goal here is to be a place of acceptance and true learning. The learning of how to be ones best self. I hope that in me learning to be better I can inspire us all to do the same.
